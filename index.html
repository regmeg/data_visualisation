Introduction

This site exhibits two visualisations of statistics on the projects run by the United States government departments and agencies. The first visualisation provides an overall view for giving a peripheral and comparative representation among agencies and their types of investments. These are categorised in Lifecycle and Planed / Actual costs and Projects lengths. The second visualisation represents a detailed view of each agency, with more internal statistics of the projects being run. These visualisations do not manifest any actual state of the affair of the United States government and are used purely for the demonstration of the D3.js library capabilities.
